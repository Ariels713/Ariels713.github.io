Two Sigma Associate

Lauren joined Two Sigma Ventures in 2018, where she provides operational and administrative support and helps manage team-wide experiences.

Prior to joining Two Sigma Ventures, Lauren was the Executive Assistant and Administrative Manager at Good Media Group, where she supported the Chief Executive Officer and Chief Strategy Officer and coordinated the Senior Leadership Retreat. She has also coordinated professional development programs and served as a project manager at The-CEI for the Teacher's Incentive Fund Grant.

La Sonrisa Foods

Lauren also a small business owner, having started La Sonrisa Empanadas with her husband in 2010. You can find them at the High Line Park from May through October. She graduated from Baruch College in 2009 with a BA in Corporate Communications.
